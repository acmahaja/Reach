R E A C H
  • WHAT IS REACH?

    • R E A C H is a webapp designed to help boost your productivity by providing resources that help you stay in control of your mental health. Meditation, mood logs, streaks, links to external help and more! It's also our entry to the HackMelbourne's Hackiethon 2021!

  • HOW DOES IT WORK?

    • With R E A C H, you can log your mood, meditate and see indicators of your mental health over time. This helps keep you productive and healthy.
